MegaGames Partners Partners

Leaders in Education

Lessons Learned Simulation & Training

LLST offers training courses and modules for humanitarian efforts with an emphasis on interactive tabletop simulations and activities.

PAXsims

PAXsims is a blog devoted to the development and effective use of games and simulation-based learning concerning issues of conflict, peacebuilding, and development in fragile and conflict-affected states, as well as to the policy application of gaming and simulation techniques.

Local Groups

CanGames

Established in 1977, CanGames is Canada’s longest-running gaming convention. Each year, hundreds of game enthusiasts meet in the Ottawa area over the course of a weekend … for fun and friendly competition. They play board games, collectible card games, miniatures games, and role-playing games.

Breakout Con

Breakout has been the best place in Toronto for tabletop gamers of all kinds to get their game on and hang out with their community since its inception in 2016.

Exploding Zebra

Exploding Zebra is an event production company in Toronto that specializes in unusual social gatherings! Is that a good thing? All signs point to maybe!

MegaGames in The World

Megagame Coalition

The Megagame Coalition is a fully volunteer organization consisting of multiple megagame groups across North America as well as dedicated individual contributors, focused on increasing MG presence at Gen Con and lowering the barrier to entry for new MG groups.

Megagame Assembly

The Megagame Assembly is a community group in the UK that runs a podcast (of the same name), maintains a blog and hosts community resources like free MGs and a calendar of upcoming games.

Megagame Makers

Megagame Makers leads a friendly community, running Megagames in the London area and acting as a focal point for Megagames across the globe, drawing on experience of over 30 years. Starting in the 1980’s, this loose association of friends has produced a great number of Megagames, always trying to improve – creating more fun and interest through original games. We are a dozen designers who share a belief in high standards and games that give a real sense of being part of history or an imagined universe.

OMEGA

OMEGA hosts and produces online megagames. From grand strategy to roleplay, economics to fireballs, our games blend serious political simulation with emergent storytelling in settings both mundane and fantastical.
