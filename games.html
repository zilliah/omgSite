Past Games

Apocalypse North

March 7, 2020 Diefenbunker Museum - 3929 Carp Rd

Apocalypse North is a game of emergency response, national survival, and federal-provincial politics during a zombie armageddon.

Undeniable Victory

October 6, 2019 Laurier House - 252 Metcalfe St

Undeniable Victory is a serious megagame built around the political, strategic, and tactical challenges of the Iran-Iraq war, 1980-1988. Still the highest-rated megagame of the decade in the UK, OMG partnered with LLST to bring to Ottawa Ben Moores, the designer of Undeniable Victory, co-host of the Last Turn Madness podcast, and one of the original Megagame Makers of the United Kingdom.

Den of Wolves

June 15, 2019 Ecclesiax Church - 2 Monk St

Den of Wolves is a Megagame about survival, politics, and paranoia as a convoy of surviving spaceships tries to stay one step ahead of their pursuers.

Watch the Skies - Cold War 2: Extraterrestrial Boogaloo

March 9, 2019 Diefenbunker Museum - 3929 Carp Rd

OMG’s take on Watch the Skies, with every part of the rules rewritten from the ground up. Two competing teams of aliens face off against Earth’s militaries, technological advances, espionage teams and (most horrifying of all) diplomatic bureaucracy.
